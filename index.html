Our Mission
Our mission is to satisfy the transportation needs of all through a utility scale network of automated low speed electric vehicles recharged by renewable technologies. So that all may have the freedom to explore, capture opportunities, and attend events of their communities; while re-establishing the bond between man and nature.

Our Vision
Push the global transportation sector to zero emissions through innovation, environmental consciousness, and efficiency.
Tyler Sonsalla
Tyler Sonsalla is a Ph.D. candidate at Louisiana Tech University in molecular sciences and nanotechnology. He has solar cell experience from his master’s degree in which he designed a transparent conductive electrode for plasmonically enhanced amorphous silicon solar cells. The experience included wiring solar cells in series and parallel, testing of individual solar cells, and analysis of solar cell results. Currently, Tyler Sonsalla is working with additive manufacturing technology through his dissertation research focused on the development of novel 3-D printable composite materials.
