Our Mission
Our mission is to satisfy the transportation needs of all through a utility scale network of automated solar rechargeable low speed electric vehicles. So that all may have the freedom to explore, capture opportunities, and attend events of their communities; while re-establishing the bond between man and nature.
Our Vision
Push the global transportation sector to zero emissions through innovation, environmental consciousness, and efficiency.
